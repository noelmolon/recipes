# molonno student breakfast sandwich


## ingredients
- Bagel
- Bacon
- Cheddar cheese
- 2 eggs
- chilies
- creme or yogurt
- 
## directions
- Cook bacon
- Cook egg in bacon grease
- Toast bagel
- Assemble bacon, egg, bagel
- blend creme and chilies together to make chipotle sauce
- add chipotle sauce to assembled sandwich.
